# Vitali — Day 1 Training Cheat Sheet

Welcome to Vitali! This 1-pager contains everything you need to know for your first day at the clinic, tailored to your role.

---

## 🙋🏽‍♀️ Recepção (Reception)
Your main goal: Manage the schedule and welcome patients.

- **Check-in patients:** Go to **Agenda**, find the appointment, and click **Aguardando** (Waiting) when they arrive.
- **New Appointments:** Click on any empty slot in the **Agenda** grid to create an appointment. Search for existing patients or create a new one on the fly.
- **WhatsApp Reminders:** The system automatically sends reminders 24h in advance. You can view replies in **Configurações > WhatsApp > Conversas**.
- **Payments:** Click on an appointment and select **Cobrar via PIX** to generate a QR Code.

## 🩺 Médico (Doctor)
Your main goal: Conduct consultations and write clinical notes.

- **Start Consultation:** Look at your **Agenda** and select the next patient marked as *Aguardando*. Click **Iniciar**.
- **Clinical Notes (SOAP):** Write your notes in the Prontuário. 
  - *Tip: If AI is enabled, you can dictate your notes, and the AI Escriba will transcribe and structure them.*
- **Prescriptions:** Go to the **Prescrições** tab, search for the medication, set dosage, and click **Salvar e Imprimir**.
- **Finish:** Click **Concluir**. This notifies Pharmacy and Billing that the consultation is done.

## 💊 Farmácia (Pharmacy)
Your main goal: Dispense medications prescribed during consultations.

- **Dispense:** When a doctor finishes a prescription, it appears in your queue under **Farmácia**. Click the prescription, gather the items, and click **Confirmar Dispensação** to automatically deduct them from the inventory.
- **Low Stock Alerts:** Check the dashboard for items below the minimum stock level.
- **Restock:** Use **Pedidos de Compra** to log incoming deliveries.

## 📄 Faturamento (Billing)
Your main goal: Generate and submit TISS guides to insurance companies.

- **Generate Guides:** After a doctor concludes an appointment, go to **Faturamento > Guias**. Verify the TUSS codes and generate the XML.
  - *Tip: If AI is enabled, the system will highlight high-risk guides to prevent rejections (glosas).*
- **Batches (Lotes):** At the end of the billing cycle, group guides into a **Lote** to send to the provider.

## ⚙️ Admin (Manager)
Your main goal: Keep the clinic running smoothly.

- **Dashboard:** Check **Analytics** for no-show rates and daily revenue.
- **Staff Management:** Add new staff in the settings. Ensure doctors have their Council (CRM/CRO) info filled out.
- **Help/Support:** Email suporte@vitali.app if any issues arise.
